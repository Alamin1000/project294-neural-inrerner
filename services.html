Our Services

Mining

Neural Internet provides AI optimization and management services for clients to participate in the Bittensor network as servers and earn rewards in TAO. Our service allows for easy integration, with options to pay for computing with resources in fiat or crypto and managed GPU rental and AI. Designed for clients who want to actively participate in the Bittensor network but lack the resources or expertise, our service is trustless, no direct access to client wallets for earned TAO.
